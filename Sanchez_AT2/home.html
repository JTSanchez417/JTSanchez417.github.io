ABOUT

ViVYD Studios is an independent entertainment studio that is devoted to produce high-quality artworks and projects like animation, comics, fiction works and etc. for the sake of storytelling. In this studio, we have a vision, and that is to create the best storytelling content of various media for potential fans to like, love, and support to the fullest.

FOUNDER: JOSEPH TROY L. SANCHEZ

A creative and imaginative mind, he is the sole founder of the studio itself, and has a knack and liking of telling stories and expressing what comes into his mind through either art, animation, or by words. He achieved a Bachelor's Degree of Multimedia Art and is currently interested in pursuing animation, writing, and sometimes, creating working artpieces that tells an artist's mind.
